How We Work

Project Management · Investment Advisory · Buyer Representation · Rentals & Sales

Our Approach

We combine experience, ethics, and attention to detail to help clients succeed at every stage of their property journey.

Consultation & Needs Analysis

We begin every relationship with a thorough consultation to understand your goals, budget, timeline, and lifestyle preferences. Whether you are buying, selling, renting, or investing—we tailor our services accordingly.

Market Intelligence

With over 25 years of experience in Catalunya, we provide insight into market trends, property values, local regulations, and emerging opportunities across Barcelona, Sitges, and the Costa Brava.

Project Management

For clients developing or renovating properties, we offer full-service project management—from site analysis and licensing to design coordination and construction supervision—ensuring timelines, budgets, and quality are met.

Buyer Representation

As your exclusive representative, we conduct property searches, due diligence, price negotiation, and coordination with legal and financial professionals—saving you time, money, and stress while protecting your interests.

Transparent Process

We maintain clear and consistent communication throughout, providing honest advice, regular updates, and detailed reporting. Our reputation is built on trust, not sales volume.

After-Sales Support

Our service doesn't stop at the signature. We continue to assist you with everything from utilities setup and decoration to rental management or resale strategy when needed.
