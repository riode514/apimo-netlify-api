Our Story

Founded on Excellence

VERV ONE was established in 1998 as Wood Side S.L., with a vision to redefine luxury real estate consultancy in Catalunya. Over 25+ years, we have evolved from a local agency into a premier international consultancy, serving discerning clients from over 30 countries.

Our journey began with a simple yet powerful commitment: to provide exceptional service that goes beyond traditional real estate transactions. Today, we stand as trusted advisors for luxury property investments across Barcelona, Sitges, and Costa Brava.

What sets us apart is our deep understanding of both local markets and international client needs. We don't just sell properties; we curate lifestyles and create lasting relationships built on trust, expertise, and results.
